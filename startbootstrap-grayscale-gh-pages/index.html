H.I.M

H.I.M. (Hourglass Inverted Maniac) is not a simple ghost. He came from a Limbo or purgatory, and he wants to destroy the "Ecos", twisted shadows of his own memories, who are wandering for the Gray Hell trying to corrupt other souls. Every time he destroys one, he recovers a fragment of his memory, but he suffers too. The way he attacks is blowing a firethrower, but it does not throw fire, it throws a black substance that consumes everything it touches.


What does he eat?

Usually, he does not eat anything, as he is a ghost, but he is known to consume the fears and despair of the souls he torments. As you can see, when he eats, he is black, and when he is not, he is white, so you can tell if he has eaten or not by his color, and the entire background change too, so be careful.

What does he like/dislike?

If you have not noticed yet, he likes to play with his victims before consuming them. One of his abilities is to create illusions, so he can make his victims see what he wants them to see, and make them feel what he wants them to feel.
What our friend dislikes is being seen, which is why he always tries to be alone, and he does not like being in very bright areas, since he hates light. That is why he has been seen very few times. It should be noted that the following illustrations are recreations, as the people who have seen it have not been able to remember it perfectly.

Strenghts and weaknesses

His strengths are basically his ability to control the minds of his victims, as well as being extremely quiet and trying to be as inconspicuous as possible. He has incredible vision thanks to his blue eye, which allows him to see his victims from an almost impossible distance. However, his weaknesses are his inability to attack things that are familiar to him, which he tries to avoid, as it reminds him of his past and makes him feel sad.

People think that he is more than 2000 years old, but nobody knows very well...
